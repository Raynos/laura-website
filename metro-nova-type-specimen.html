METRO NOVA
TYPE SPECIMEN

The Metro Nova Type Specimen concept is based around the quote
"A masterpiece lost, found and reimagined". The user has to discover the letter forms themselves by matching up the two sets of cards. Each card showcases a letter of the alphabet in one of the 26 weights.
