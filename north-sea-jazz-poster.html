NORTH SEA
JAZZ FESTIVAL

Artposter

Jazz is unpredictable and jazz is well thought out. Jazz is abstract and jazz is clear. This photographic poster shows the special contrasting elements of the music.

The design was part of a yearly design brawl for the North Sea Jazz festival in Rotterdam and was displayed among other competitors during an exhibition beforehand.
